         7. Red Flags (Critical Overrides)
● ☐ Snap cap (return from injury/conditioning)
● ☐ Late downgrade / DNP
● ☐ OL injuries materially change outlook
● ☐ Severe weather for the prop type (wind/rain for passing/kicking; field for rushing)
● ☐ Game-script risk opposite of prop
● ☐ Market steam against (≥2.5 yards or ≥20¢) without new info
● ☐ Role change / committee shift
● ☐ Key teammate injury changes defensive attention (e.g., WR1 out)

Hard Gates (Auto-PASS or Re-route)
● Coverage/Protection Gate: (Opp Pressure% − PBWR_adj) ≥ 8% AND CB room top-10 AND press/man ≥30% → PASS WR/QB overs; TE only if no bracket; upgrade RB receiving.
● Primary Receiver Constraint: Bracket ≥35% AND ΔT2T ≤ −80ms → Reduce target share −4–8 pts and cap aDOT −1.0–1.5; many WR/TE overs become PASS.
● QB Rush/Contain Gate: Spy ≥12% OR (Zone ≥65% + top-10 contain) → Scramble expectation −20–35%; fade QB rush overs unless designed runs ≥2/gm.
● OL Continuity Gate: ≤3 healthy starters → PASS QB/WR overs; TE only if not held in protection.
● Checkdown Funnel Rule: Gate hits + CB lock → Upgrade RB receiving overs; downshift outside-WR deep/long.
● Kicker Philosophy Gate: High go-rate + high RZ TD% → Fade FGM overs; TD props = boom/bust.
● Injury Contingency: If X exits → Y benefits/Z loses (pre-write redistribution trees).
● Scheme Collision Gate: (auto-PASS or heavy downgrade)
• Trigger (any 1 strong or 2 medium):
– Run: Offense outside-zone ≥55% vs defense top-8 vs outside-zone OR offense gap/power ≥55% vs defense top-8 vs gap/power.
– Pass—Vertical/Deep PA: Offense PA/deep-shot rate ≥30% with T2T ≥2.8s vs defense sim/creeper ≥20% + press/man ≥30% + pressure ≥ league avg.
– Pass—RPO/Quick: Offense RPO/quick-game ≥45% vs defense match-quarters/robber ≥30% + squat/press ≥30%.
– Empty: Offense empty ≥15% vs defense blitz/sim vs empty ≥35%.
• Action:
– RB rush overs: −10–20% expectation if run collision; auto-PASS if PBWR_adj gap is also bad.
– WR/TE overs: cap aDOT −0.8–1.2; target share −3–6 pts if vertical-PA collision; many become PASS.
– QB pass overs: reduce explosive% and yards; lean UNDER/PASS.
– QB rush overs: if vertical collision + contain/spy present, treat as QB Rush/Contain Gate.
– RB receiving: upgrade when pass collisions imply checkdown funnel.

                  11. Data Dictionary (Universal)
● YPRR / aDOT / YAC: Receiving efficiency & depth indicators.
● EPA/play / Success rate: Drive-sustaining efficiency; context for matchup fit.
● Explosive rate: % plays over big-gain thresholds (10+ rush, 15+ pass).
● Run-/Pass-block win rate: OL vs DL ability to create/hold lanes.
● Pressure/Blitz/P2S%: Pass rush environment affecting QBs/WRs/TEs.
● Tackle share/ADT: IDP volume and role indicators.
● Market steam: Rapid, multi-book moves often tied to fresh info.
● ΔT2T: Change in QB time-to-throw vs season baseline (ms); negative means faster decisions/shorter routes.
● PBWR_adj: Pass-block win rate adjusted for opponent strength and chip/help rate.
● Bracket rate: % snaps primary pass-catcher is doubled/bracketed.
● Spy rate / Contain rank: % snaps defense assigns QB spy; contain quality rank (top-10 indicates strong edge discipline).
● Checkdown funnel: Pressure + coverage combo that shifts targets from WRs to RBs (screens, flats, arrows).
● Scheme collision: Structural mismatch between core offensive concepts (OZ/gap, deep PA, RPO/quick, empty) and defense counters (top run fits, sim/creeper, match-quarters/robber, blitz vs empty).
