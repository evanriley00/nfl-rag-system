8) UDSS Scoring (Universal)
0–2 ✅ SAFE | 3–5 ⚠️ RISKY | 6+ ❌ DANGEROUS. Any red‑flag override = ❌
A. Form vs Line (0–3 pts)
                                       * Season median vs line: ≥ line ☐ (0) | 0.5–2 under ☐ (+1) | ≥2.5 under ☐ (+2)

                                       * Last‑5 median vs line: ≥ line ☐ (0) | 0.5–2 under ☐ (+1) | ≥2.5 under ☐ (+2)

                                       * Volume stability (snaps/routes/attempts): Stable ☐ (0) | Variable ☐ (+1)

B. Role & Leverage (0–3 pts)
                                          * Primary role ☐ (0) | Split ☐ (+1) | Secondary ☐ (+2)

                                          * High‑leverage share (RZ/inside‑5/1st‑read): Strong ☐ (0) | Mixed ☐ (+1) | Weak ☐ (+2)

C. Matchup Fit (0–3 pts)
                                             * Defense vs prop type: Favorable ☐ (0) | Neutral ☐ (+1) | Tough ☐ (+2)

                                             * OL vs Front‑7 or Coverage fit: Edge O ☐ (0) | Even ☐ (+1) | Edge D ☐ (+2)

D. Environment & Market (0–2 pts)
                                                * Script/weather supportive and no steam vs you: Yes ☐ (0) | Mixed ☐ (+1) | Bad ☐ (+2)

11) Data Dictionary (Universal)
                                                         * YPRR / aDOT / YAC: Receiving efficiency & depth indicators.

                                                         * EPA/play / Success rate: Drive‑sustaining efficiency; context for matchup fit.

                                                         * Explosive rate: % plays over big‑gain thresholds (10+ rush, 15+ pass).

                                                         * Run‑/Pass‑block win rate: OL vs DL ability to create/hold lanes.

                                                         * Pressure/Blitz/P2S%: Pass rush environment affecting QBs/WRs/TEs.

                                                         * Tackle share/ADT: IDP volume and role indicators.

                                                         * Market steam: Rapid, multi‑book moves often tied to fresh info.
