# Chiefs Offense Overview

Kansas City continues to lean on motion, spacing, and quarterback improvisation to create explosive plays. The offense is most dangerous when the passing game forces safeties to rotate late and linebackers to communicate through bunch or stack releases.

When defenses sit in soft zone, the short passing game becomes an extension of the run. Quick outs, option routes, and crossers allow the quarterback to stay on schedule while still threatening yards after catch.

The biggest stress point for defenses is handling layered route combinations. If the flat defender widens too quickly, the intermediate window opens. If the defense squeezes inside, the offense takes easy completions outside the numbers.
