# Wide Receiver Scouting Template

Evaluate wide receivers on release package, route tempo, separation at the break point, ball tracking, catch consistency, and run-after-catch vision.

Against zone-heavy defenses, awareness and pacing are often as valuable as pure speed. Receivers who can settle in space and present clean throwing windows raise quarterback efficiency.

Against man-heavy defenses, burst, leverage control, and late hands become more important. Physical corners can disrupt timing unless the receiver has a clear counter at the line of scrimmage.
